Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a general service take?

+

A general service typically takes around 2 hours. This includes oil change, filter replacement, fluid top-up, spark plug check, and battery inspection. The exact time may vary based on your bike's condition.

What's your refund policy?

+

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service for any reason, we'll refund your entire payment. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you provide warranty on your work?

+

Yes! All our services come with comprehensive warranty coverage. If any issue arises within the warranty period due to our work, we'll fix it for free.

Are you available throughout Hyderabad?

+

Yes! We're available throughout Hyderabad for home service. Just provide your address during booking, and our technician will come to your location at the scheduled time.

How do I track my booking?

+

Click the "Track Booking" button, enter your mobile number, and you'll see real-time updates including technician details, current location, and estimated arrival time.

+

Do you use genuine parts?

+

Absolutely! We only use genuine, original parts for all replacements. This ensures your bike performs optimally and maintains its value.

Can I view my previous bookings?

Yes! We keep a complete history of all your services. You can view your previous bookings anytime by contacting us or using the Track Booking feature with your phone number.
